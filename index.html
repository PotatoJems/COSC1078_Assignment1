My Three test Results

The following are results taken from three personality tests. They are the Myers Briggs Type Inidcator tests, a learning preferences test, and a test to guage my level of creativity, below the results is a overall perception of them.

Myers Briggs Type Indicator

From going through an MBTI test, I was able to find out that my personality is reflective of The Entertainer (specifically ESFP-A).

Strengths

Entertainers are known for being able to step out of their comfort zones in full confidence, striving for originality, injecting artistic creativity into their words and actions, show great pleasure in being proactive, recognising the subtle changes around them, and portraying great people skills around strangers and those they love.

Weaknesses

On the other hand, entertainers are known for being very emotional, becoming vulnerable in the face of criticism, ignoring and avoiding conflict entirely only to find funner occasions, having a high tendency for boredom, having fragile long-term plans which show flaws, and generally being unfocused towards long-term dedications.

Learning Preferences

From going through this test, I was able to figure out that I am a Visual learner, they work best when provided with diagrams or other visual stimuli, they may also find pleasure in perceiving what they are learning in their mind. Visual learners have the biggest gripe with verbal directions and have a tendency to be distracted by short, ultimately meaningless sounds.

Creativity Test

From going through this test, I was able to figure out that my creativity is at a level that is a "work in progress", though it is very close to being one of my core strengths, I'd just have to adapt and present my creative ideas to more potential opportunities, my exact results are as follows:

  • Finding Problems (Preparation) = 2.4/5
