ExactBot, the FAQ chatbot!

Overview

ExactBot is an FAQ chatbot which can be adapted to more than a few contexts, it will reside in the discussion forums of many different styles of informative websites, anything from recipe sites, to car sites, and many more. Companies and other website developers will be able to download and apply ExactBot to their discussion forms or FAQ pages, making the application process easy. Developing the chatbot will take two to three years, and it will require complex coding for it to run effectively. Regardless, its benefit will aid several people browsing the internet, proving that the effort to program ExactBot is worth it.

Motivation

The reason I hope to bring this project to light is due to its fortified utility. Across the internet, there are 4 billion users that find themselves confused over a certain topic or just would like some insight about a subject they are interested in. Using high-level Python code will be a tough yet effective solution to this issue. ExactBot will be the solution to many, if not all of your immediate questions about a website.

Description

Exactbot is able to help answer so many questions thanks to its vast vocabulary, and it’s succinct customizability with its phrases. The chatbot will be able to respond to so many different questions thanks to its machine learning which can make distinctions between separate types of queries which may have the same general theme. For example, say you were on a recipe website and were asking about flour, if you ask ExactBot “What type of flour should I use?”, it will find “flour”, “type”, and then the query of “What” and will be able to respond quickly with a predetermined answer that is assigned to the question. Now imagine you asked a second question of “How much flour should be used?”, it will see the “flour”, “much”, the query of “How”, make the distinction that the question is asking for an amount of flour, and respond with a pre-built answer. ExactBot is a downloadable program, meaning that its exact operations will function by a website developer, they would start by selecting a query, which could be “What”, “Where”, “When”, “How”, “Will”, etc., and then a word that contextualises the question, like “style”, “much”, “type” which are loosely connected to the query, and then an indicator, “flour”, “car”, “dog”, etc. which can essentially be anything thanks Exactbot’s ability to add any of these types of words, essentially customising the database to their liking.

The developer would then type an answer to the three chosen words, giving them the full reign to decide how ExactBot will respond to users’ questions. When looking through the dropdown menus for your needed word, you will be able to search for the word you’re looking for, whether it’s a query word, a word tied to the query, or an indicator. If you cannot find the words you are looking for, you can always add your own to the database (which will remain private since you are applying it to your website) or wait for a subsequent update to ExactBot. Future updates to ExactBot will eventually remove the need for the website developer to type out the answer, and will apply machine learning to autofill a response which could then be refined by the developer, think of it as a less aggressive form of autocorrect software.

Tools and Technologies

For ExactBot to work effectively, it will need high-level Python coding for the best results. Opening up the program will bring you to an initial page where you type in the URL for your discussion forum, you will then be greeted to the page where you can customise and answer questions (as shown in Description), then you will have total freedom at that point. ExactBot is powered by the open source tool of PandoraBots, the largest and most experienced chatbot hosting service on the interne
